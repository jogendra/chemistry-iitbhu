Dr. M. A. Quraishi

About Me

I am presently serving as Professor and Head Department of Chemistry in Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University). I obtain B.Sc. (1969) degree and M.Sc. (1971) degree from Saugar University, Saugar. M.Phil (1978) and Ph.D (1986) from Kurukshetra University. D.Sc. (2004) from centrally funded AMU Aligarh. Before joining as Professor at BHU, I was Reader (1990-2005) at AMU Aligarh.
