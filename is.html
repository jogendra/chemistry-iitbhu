Research Interests

Chemistry of nanomaterials
  • Noble metal, metal oxides and alloy nanoparticles; synthesis and characterization
  • LSPR behavior and SAXS analysis
  • Toxic heavy metal removal by nanoadsorbents
  • Kinetics and mechanism of nanocatalysed reactions.
Computational Physical Chemistry
  • Monte Carlo/Molecular Dynamics nanoparticle interactions simulation
  • Surface catalyzed reactions: Kinetic Monte Carlo simulations
  • Oscillatory reactions and non-equilibrium phase transitions
Computational Materials Science
  • Kinetics of first order phase transition (KJMA approach)
